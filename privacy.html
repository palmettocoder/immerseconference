Privacy Policy


Your privacy is critically important to us!


It is The Banner Church’s policy to respect your privacy regarding any information we may collect while operating our websites. This Privacy Policy applies only to information we collect through the our websites and does not apply to our collection of information from other sources.
This Privacy Policy, together with our Terms and Conditions posted on our sites, set forth the general rules and policies governing your use of our sites. Depending on your activities when visiting our websites, you may be required to agree to additional terms and conditions. Website Visitors
Like most website operators, The Banner Church collects non-personally-identifying information of the sort that web browsers and servers typically make available, such as the browser type, language preference, referring site, and the date and time of each visitor request. From time to time, The Banner Church may release non-personally-identifying information in the aggregate, e.g., by publishing a report on trends in the usage of its websites.
The Banner Church also collects potentially personally-identifying information like Internet Protocol (IP) addresses for logged in users. The Banner Church only discloses logged in user IP addresses under the same circumstances that it uses and discloses personally-identifying information as described below.

Cookies


To enrich and perfect your online experience, The Banner Church uses “Cookies”, similar technologies and services provided by others to display personalized content, appropriate advertising and store your preferences on your computer.
A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor’s computer, and that the visitor’s browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. The Banner Church uses cookies to help The Banner Church identify and track visitors, their usage of https://www.immersegvl.com, and their website access preferences. The Banner Church visitors who do not wish to have cookies placed on their computers should set their browsers to refuse cookies before using The Banner Church’s websites, with the drawback that certain features of The Banner Church’s websites may not function properly without the aid of cookies.
By continuing to navigate our website without changing your cookie settings, you hereby acknowledge and agree to The Banner Church’s use of cookies.
